Projects & Experiences

Unimed de Goiânia

2022 - Currently

Currently, I work at Unimed de Goiânia, where I am responsible for the data engineering of a comprehensive data warehouse. This project encompasses several critical areas of the company, each represented by a specific data mart. Here are the areas integrated into our data warehouse:

  1. Authorization Audit

    I developed a data mart for the Authorization Audit, which allows detailed monitoring and analysis of authorization processes, ensuring compliance and operational efficiency.

  2. Medical Production

    The Medical Production data mart focuses on tracking the productivity of healthcare professionals, providing insights into performance and service quality.

  3. Controllership

    In the Controllership area, the data mart facilitates financial management and control, providing accurate data for strategic and operational decision-making.

  4. Billing

    The Billing data mart consolidates information on billing and payments, helping to optimize revenue flow and improve the company's financial management.

  5. Portfolio Profile

    I developed a data mart to analyze the Portfolio Profile of clients, allowing a deep understanding of the characteristics and behaviors of beneficiaries.

  6. Portfolio Health

    This data mart focuses on Portfolio Health, monitoring health indicators and service utilization patterns, helping to promote better care and prevention practices.

  7. Accredited Network Profile

    The Accredited Network Profile data mart facilitates the analysis of the network of service providers, evaluating performance and efficiency to ensure a high-quality network.

  8. Medical Regulation

    In Medical Regulation, I created a data mart that assists in managing regulatory processes, ensuring that practices are aligned with current norms and regulations.

  9. Accounting Loss Ratio

    The Accounting Loss Ratio data mart enables the monitoring of claims expenses, helping to identify trends and manage financial risks more effectively.

Integral Médica

2024 - Currently

At Integral Médica, I am currently working on developing a Customer Data Platform (CDP) to store both internal and external data. This includes data from social media platforms such as LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok. The goal is to link individuals from social media with the company's internal data, creating a comprehensive and unified view of our customers. This integration allows for more targeted marketing strategies, personalized customer experiences, and improved data-driven decision-making.
